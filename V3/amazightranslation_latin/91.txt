91
tafukt
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi ad ggalɣ s tafukt d uznẓaṛ nns
2 d wayyur iɣ t id iṭṭfaṛ
3 d wass iɣ t id issugga
4 d yiḍ iɣ t idl
5 d ignna d ma tt isskin
6 d wakal, d ma tt ifsmn
7 d iman, d ma tt isastwan
8 iml as aɣaras ifrɣn d wad yuɣdn
9 ar ijjnjm ṛbbi wad isɣusn ixf nns
10 ɣik lli issukl ɣwalli t inxxln
11 ha nn tsskir gis **tamud** amazan nnsn, s uẓḍam nnsn
12 illiɣ ayttazzal wacc nnsn, iskr ɣaylli xf d msacka?
13 inna asn umazan n ṛbbi: ad t igan d **ṣalḥ** ha nn ad ur tslim taṛamt n ṛbbi d tissi nns g wass nns?
14 macc sskirkksn t, aywṛn nɣin taṛamt, ɣik ann a xf tn isrfufnṛbbi nnsn s ubkkaḍ nnsn, yaywa isgun fllasn tammara, ur gisn injm mqqar d yan
15 acku ṛbbi ur ikṣuḍ, ma rad d iggru i ɣik ann? nɣ d ɣwa lli inɣan taṛamt, ur nn itam ma rad iggru?
