96
tuwṭṭat
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna jibril: i sidna* **muḥmmad** {taẓallit n ṛbbi fllas} aywa ɣr s yism n ṛbbi nnk, lli iskrn kra illan
2 iskr afgan g tuwṭṭat n idammn
3 aywa ɣr, ɣik lli tɣrid tamzwarut, ha nn ṛbbi nnk yuf kra yakkan
4 ɣwalli immaln i ufgan s tirra n tazzit, nɣ d aɣnbub: **lqalam**?
5 imla i ufgan ɣaylli ur issin
6 ɛad, ha nn afgan ar izray awttu? iɣal is idus?
7 iɣal ixf nns is izza xf ṛbbi nns, s mad iṭṭaf?
8 awa, ha nn tawrrayt nnk a afgan tra nn dar ṛbbi nnk?
9 awa, ini iyi: manik nn srk iga ɣwalli issugin mad ifulkin? ad t igan, d **abu jahl**: anttay n umazan
10 issugi i ismg n ṛbbi ad t igan d umazan ad ittẓalla?
11 ini iyi: is ur imun ismg d uɣaras n ṛbbi?
12 nɣ ad immal tikṣṣaḍ n ṛbbi?
13 ini iyi, iɣ isskarkks yaywa yanf i uɣaras n ṛbbi
14 maxx is ur issin ṛbbi nns? is issn kra iskr?
15 maxx? tagallit iɣ tt ur igda g mad iskar, a sar t njjikr s twunza nns ɣ iggi n lafiyt
16 ad tt igan d twunza n **abu jahl** lli iskarn tamsskirkkist tg tanabakaḍt
17 inna ṛbbi: ini as ad iɣr i ayt dars lli s inna iṭṭaf tn
18 inna ṛbbi: iɣ asn iɣra, ha nn rad nɣr i inirn n urwass ad t zbin
19 uhu, ha nn ad as ur tsgdd, macc aywa ar ttẓallad ar tskard ma k izzaẓn s ṛbbi nnk
