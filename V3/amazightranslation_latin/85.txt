85
itran
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi: ad ggalɣ s ignna lli ɣ llan itran
2 d wass lli s yusy ṛbbi, nnan iga t wass n tɣrɣart?
3 d wass inigi, nnan iga t wass n uṣiḍyaṣ (**ljamɛ**) d wass lli f gan inagan, nnan iga t wass n **ɛaṛafa** macc ṛbbi ka ad issnn
4 inna ṛbbi: ggulliɣ ar tturgamn ayt usaru
5 ad t igan d lafiyt (lɛfit) lli gis irɣan
6 aywan skkusn ɣ tma (tama) nns ar t tettmnadn
7 sul gin inagan f mad skarn i inaflasn
8 ha nn ir tn mrrtn bla f illiɣ flsn s ṛbbi lli igan anddabu amttugaḍay
9 lli mu tga tgldit n ignwan d wakal tinns, hann ṛbbi iga amannay n kra illan
10 bann ɣwilli srfufunnin inaflasn, d tnaflasin, ɣaman ur d iwrrin s uɣaras n ṛbbi, ha nn tlla asn tmmara n tmẓla, ɣiklli asn tlla tmmara n ukmmuḍ
11 amma ɣwilli flsnin aywan skrn mad ifulkin, hann llant asn turtitin lli mu ngin isaffn ɣ ddawat nnsnt, ɣikann ad igan amrkid anmɣur
12 hann tayti n ṛbbi nnk tga tassast f imnabaln
13 hann ntta ar iskar afgan inɣi t, yaywa issudr t id
14 ntta ad igan amssaruf amiri
15 dars yulɣn
16 ar iskar kra ira
17 ntta ṛbbi: is k id yucka unɣmis n izmmaln?
18 ad t igan d **firɛawn** d **tamud** lli nsrfufn
19 nɣ d ɣwilli nblnin llan ɣ turda?
20 awa, hann ṛbbi issutl asn d ɣ kraygat tasga
21 hann tabrat ad tga **lqʷṛan** issusmn
22 ittyara ɣ dar ṛbbi s ɣmklli d dis yuckan?
