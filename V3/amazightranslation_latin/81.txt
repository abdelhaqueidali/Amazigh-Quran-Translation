81
amuttl
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi: iɣ tmmuttl tafukt, tns tifawt nns?
2 d iɣ lluzzan itran
3 d iɣ akufn idrarn ɣ udɣar nnsn
4 d tramin iɣ ttunnxalnt, waxxa lant atig?
5 d taywalin iɣ munnt, ɣ kra igan anaw, zun d taɣaḍt d uccn?
6 d iɣ flufan igrwaln: (**lbiḥaṛ**)
7 d iman iɣ ttuẓlayn kraygat anaw imun d mad t irwasn
8 d tazzant iɣ ttusqsa?
9 mad igan abkkaḍ nns aylliɣ ttumḍal tsul nit sul?
10 d iɣ ttufsamn warratn, n kra iskr ufgan
11 d iɣ immatti ignna ɣ udɣar nns?
12 d iɣ tfdaddi tmẓla n urwass
13 d iɣ d taẓ turtit n ṛbbi s dar inaflasn
14 akud ann kraygat yan issn mad iskr ɣ tudrt nns?
15 inna ṛbbi: ad ggalɣ s itran iɣ iwrrin
16 lli igan itran iɣ ntln
17 d yiḍ iɣ d izzugaz s tillas nns nɣ iwrri
18 d taylgi iɣ tfaw
19 amki iga **lqʷṛan** bla tinnit n umazan iṛwan, ad t igan d **jibril**
20 iga amadwas iṛwan ɣ dar ṛbbi
21 ittusgad ɣ dar ṛbbi: ɣik lli ijjnjum kra d yiwi
22 amki iga umddakkʷl nnun amṣuḍ, zɛma **muḥammad**: {taẓallit n ṛbbi fllas}
23 tagallit ar iẓṛṛa umazan **jibril** ɣ uflla ifawn
24 ha nn **muḥammad** {taẓallit n ṛbbi fllas} ur ay issntal amya ɣ **lqʷṛan**
25 ɣik lli ur igi **lqʷṛan** ttinnit n iblis itturgamn
26 awa, mani s rad anfn iswingimn nnsn fln **lqʷṛan**?
27 ha nn ntta ur igi bla assukti i kra illan
28 ɣik ann d wanna gigun iran ad imun d uɣaras n ṛbbi
29 ha nn ur rad tirim kra, bla iɣ ira ṛbbi, lli igan ṛbbi n kra illan
