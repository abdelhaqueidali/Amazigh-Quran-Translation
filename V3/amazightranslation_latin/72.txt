72
amdun
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi: ittuzan iyi d: is tssfld yat tribbut ɣ umdun, nnan: hann nkkni nssfld i **lqʷṛan** issusmn
2 ar immal mad ifulkin, naywa nfls srs, hann ur sar rad nskr i ṛbbi nɣ amadraw
3 hann tattuy tummɣri n ṛbbi nnɣ, ur dars tamddakklt ula arraw
4 hann is ka ttinin imṣuḍn nnɣ azkkr f ṛbbi
5 hann nkkni nɣal is ur sar rad inna ufgan d umdun tikrkas f ṛbbi?
6 hann kkan tt inn irgazn ɣ ufgan ar tlɣasn s irgazn ɣ umdun, imil rnun asn asxnuns
7 hann nttni ɣaln ɣiklli tɣalm is ur rad d sar issudr ṛbbi mqqar d yan iɣ immut?
8 inna umdun: hann nkkni nɣli aylliɣ naẓ ignna, macc nufa t inn iktar s imḥḍan dusnin d imrruyn
9 hann nkkni nkka tt inn ar gis nttskkuyyus ɣ man nn yaẓn issfld, macc ɣilad wanna issflidn ar nn ittafa amrruy imdi as
10 hann nkkni ur nssin is d uẓluḍ, a rad yut kra illan f iggi n wakal? nɣ d is srsn ira ṛbbi nnsn mad ifulkin?
11 hann nkkni llan gignɣ wid rwanin, ɣiklli gignɣ llan mad tn ur igin, acku nga tirubba bḍanin
12 hann nkkni nssn is ur rad sar nkk f ṛbbi ɣ iggi n wakal, ɣiklli ur rad t sar nssṛmi iɣ as nrwul
13 hann nkkni lliɣ nssfld i **lqʷṛan** naywa nfls srs, acku wanna iflsn s ṛbbi nns, hann ur sar ikṣuḍ amnnurf, ula asxnuns
14 hann illan gignɣ mad igan **imuslmn** ɣiklli gignɣ imla mad igan inẓḍamn, ɣwilli ganin **imuslmn** hann gʷran (gran) afulki
15 amma ɣwilli ganin inẓḍamn, hann gan ikccuḍn n urwass
16 hann mtad is umẓn aɣaras n ṛbbi, a sar tn nsswa s waman ggutnin
17 fad ad tn srsn narm, hann wanna yugin awal n ṛbbi nns, rad t nsiki f tmmara iggutn
18 hann timzgidiwin gant tinn ṛbbi, hann ad gisnt ur taqqram i bla i ṛbbi
19 hann lliɣ ibidd ismg n ṛbbi, yaywa ar as yaqqra, alayn (ɛlayn) ad fllas gudin imdunn
20 ini: hann a mu ak aqqraɣ d ṛbbi inu, hann ur as skirɣ amadraw ɣ mqqar d yan
21  ini: hann nkki ur awn ssugirɣ amya ɣ tawaɣit ula tanafut
22 ini: hann ur sar rad iyi ikks ɣ ufus n ṛbbi mqqar d yan, ula rad sar afɣ mani s ad ttanfɣ bla s dars
23 bla asslkm zɣ dar ṛbbi d tbratin nns, hann wanna imzarayn d ṛbbi d umazan nns, hann tqqan t id lafiyt (lɛfit) n urwass rad bdda gis zdɣn
24 ard iɣ ẓṛan ɣaylli s ad tn nssiwid, akud ann rad ssnn, man wanna mu tga tannilt nns ajɛniḍ, d ma mu idrus ugdud nns?
25 ini: hann ur ssinɣ is d yaẓ ɣaylli s akʷn (akn) nssiwid? nɣ d is rad awn t inn issaggug ṛbbi inu?
26 ṛbbi ad igan amassan n untal, hann ur ad immal antal nns i mqqar d yan
27 bla wanna f iga adgi ɣ imazann, ɣwann ar dis yismun (ismuna) ṛbbi ma tt iḥḍḍun ɣ kraygat tasga ard isslkm tabrat n ṛbbi
28 fad ad sslkmn tibratin n ṛbbi nnsn, hann issn ɣikann gisn, acku issutl d i kra darsn illan, ɣiklli issn kra illan, yan s yan
