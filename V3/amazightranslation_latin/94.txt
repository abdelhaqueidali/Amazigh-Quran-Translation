94
ammuzzu
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi i umazan nns: maxx is ak ur nsmmuzi admr nnk ur gis kra n tkrẓi? nktur t s tmllawt
2 inna as ṛbbi: naywa nsitti fllak abkkaḍ nnk, zɛma ur sul fllak illi mqqar d amya, g mad izwarn, ula mad iggʷran
3 lli iẓẓayn xf tadawt nnk, zɛma  mad tusid
4 inna as ṛbbi: nsattuy ak abdar nnk, d watig nnk, kra g ttubdarɣ, ha nn ttubdard gis kyyin
5 inna ṛbbi: ha nn kra iga t tikṛẓi tlla dis tafsast, acku tafsast tga bdda xf tkṛẓi
6 ha nn tikṛẓi tlla dis tafsast ɣik lli s inna ṛbbi
7 inna ṛbbi: iɣ tfrid taẓallit nnk, nɣ d taɣawsa n ddunit aywa sṭṭufuṛ as, ma rad ak irwu grak d ṛbbi nnk
8 tsmun tn tayri nnk g ufus n ṛbbi nnk
