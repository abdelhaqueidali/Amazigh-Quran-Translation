98
umlan
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, ur gin ɣwilli nblnin g ayt udlis, d imnabaln wid nfassaynin aylliɣ tn id yucka umlan: ad t igan d **lquṛan** nɣ d amazan
2 ntta ad yuzn ṛbbi issaqqra arratn ɣusnin, ad tn igan d **lquṛan**
3 llan gisn aylan fawnin
4 ha nn ur bḍin ɣwilli mu ittufka udlis, bla ḍaṛat n mad asn d yucka umlan: zɛma **lquṛan** d umazan
5 ha nn ur asn ittunni bla ad ssumdn s ṛbbi, aywan zddign as asgd, munn d uɣaras n ṛbbi ar ttẓallan, ar akkan takuti, acku ɣik ann ad igan asgd yuɣdn
6 ha nn ɣwilli nblnin ɣ ayt udlis, d ɣwilli skrnin amadraw n ṛbbi, kcmn arwass, zdɣn bdda gis. ɣwinn ad igan uẓuḍ n ufgan
7 amma ɣwilli flsnin aywan skrn mad ifulkin, ɣwilli nn nttnin ad igan tufut n ufgan
8 inna ṛbbi: tiɣrad nnsn g dar ṛbbi nnsn g wass n tɣrɣart, gant tnt turtitin n **ɛadn** lli mu ngin isaffn g ddawat nnsnt, aywan zdɣn bdda gisnt, ha nn ɣwinn isdgi fllasn ṛbbi, ɣik lli fllasn sdgin ula nttni, f ɣaylli asn ifka, inna ṛbbi ha nn ɣik ann ila i wanna ikṣuḍn ṛbbi nns
