109
imnabaln
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi i umazan nns: aywa, bḍu, disn, ini asn: wa imnabaln
2 ha nn nkkin ur rad ssumdɣ s ɣaylli s tssumdm, zɛma iduman nnun?
3 ɣik ann d kʷnnin, ur ad tssumdm s ma ssumdɣ nkkin, ad t igan d ṛbbi
4 ula nkkin,  ha nn ur ad ssumdɣ s ɣaylli s tssumdm kʷnnin
5 ɣik ann d kʷnnin,  ha nn ur ad tssumdm s ma s ssumdɣ nkkin
6 ha nn tṭṭafm asgd nnun,  ad t igan d unabal, ɣik lli ṭṭafɣ nkkin asgd inu,  ad t igan d **lislam**
