taɣrɣart 3
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, 1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, taɣrɣart lli d iqqann, lliɣ rad yaggʷ kra ɣ skrn tikrkas
2 aḥḥ manik tga tɣrɣart ann iɣ d tugga!? hann isrfufn n ṛbbi kra tt isskirksn
3 man wanna rad ak iml taɣrɣart ann lli d iqqann?
4 hann **tamud**d **ɛad** sskirkksn taɣrɣart nns rad tili
5 amma **tamud** hann rfufunn s tɣuyyit
6 amma **ɛad** bann rfufunn s kigan n waḍu isɣuyyin
7 igr t fllasn ṛbbi sa waḍan, d tam ussan zdinin, s ar ttannit middn ḍṛan gis zun d igjwan n igujjaf mmarnin
8 awa, is asn jju tẓlit agaluz?
9 yucka d **firɛawn** d mad t izwarn, d ayt iɣrman s ubkkaḍ
10 aywan mmzrin d umazan n ṛbbi nnsn, ɣikann a x tn yumẓ yan wammaẓ yussn
11 hann aylliɣ attuyn waman, nusi kn (kʷn) f igi (iggi) n tanawt
12 fad ad awn gis nskr asskti, ad t ḥsun imzgan (imzzaɣ) ssflidnin
13 hann iɣ ittuṣwaḍ ɣ isk yan uṣwaḍ
14 siɣ ittyasay wakal d idrarn nmiqqirn gratsn, aywan qrssin s yat tkklit
15 hann akud ann (kudann) tlkm tɣrɣart
16 s istɣ ignna acku akud ann (kudann) ilmḍ
17 s bddn (biddn) inirn ɣ tsggiwin (tsigiwin) n ignwan, aywan asn ttam gisn adabu n ṛbbi nnk ɣ wass ann (ɣass ann) f iggi (igi) n umgṛaḍ (umgraḍ) nnsn
18 ɣ wass ann (ɣass ann) a rad tgim ddrst, fad ad tḥsum, ur ad gigun intl mqqar d umuya (amya) ɣ ɣaylli tskrm
19 inna ṛbbi: hann wanna mu ittufka udlis nns ɣ ufus nns afasi ar ittini: aywa amẓat ɣrat adlis inu ifulkin
20 hann nkki kkiɣ tt inn ɣ tudrt inu, ssnɣ is rad ttusqsiɣ
21 inna ṛbbi: ɣwad, hann illa ɣ waddad ifjjijn
22 ɣ turtit yattuyn
23 aẓway n tẓṛmin (tẓrmin) nns yaẓ d afus bla takrrayt
24 ar asn ittyawnna (ittunni): aywa ar cttayat ar tssam, hann urɣud awn s ɣaylli tzzrim ɣ ussan zrinin ɣ tudrt
25 amma wanna mu ittufka udlis nns ɣ ufus nns aẓlmaḍ, hann ar ittini: mra hlli ur iyi ittufka (ittufki) udlis inu?
26 ula ssnɣ ma ɣ rad ttusqsiɣ
27 mra hlli tga tmttant (**lmut**) awttu gr iyi d tudrt ad?
28 acku ur flla yusy wayada inu mqqar d amya
29 ɣiklli iy tfl tdusi inu
30 kudnna ad ittunna: aywa, amẓat t, krfat t
31 tawim t tgrm (tluḥm) t inn ɣ uẓiẓn
32 aywa, ṛẓiyat (rẓiyat) t ɣ ssfud n uzzal, tiɣzi nns, illa gis sa id mraw (samraw niɣ d kṛaḍ wagnarn d mraw) n iɣil
33 hann ikka tt inn ur iflis s ṛbbi mqqurn
34 ɣiklli ur ad ikkat takrrayt f umttcu n imẓlaḍ
35 hann ɣ wass ad (ɣass ad) ur dars ɣ ɣid amddakkl
36 ula tirmt bla ɣ uṛṣḍ (urṣḍ) lli ingin ɣ ayt urwass
37 ur ad t ictta bla imnabaln
38 inna ṛbbi: ad ggalɣ s mad ttmnadm
39 ula mad ur ttmnadm
40 amki iga **lqʷṛan** ad bla tinnit n umazan iṛwan (irwan)
41 hann ur igi urarn n umarir, macc idrus ad tflsm
42 ula iga awal n ugzzan, macc idrus ad tkttim
43 hann ad iga d uguz zɣ dar ṛbbi n kra illan
44 hann mtad is fllaɣ iskr kra n tkrkas
45 a sar t nsrfufn s tdusi nnɣ
46 sul nbbi as aẓaṛ (aẓar)n ul
47 mqqar d yan gigun ur iẓḍaṛ (iẓḍar) ad fllas irar
48 hann **lqʷṛan** iga asskti n ɣwilli kṣuḍnin ṛbbi
49 hann nkkni nnsn is gigun llan imsskirkksn
50 hann iga tamgṛaẓt (tamgraẓt) i imnabaln
51 hann iga inzgi ɣ ur tlli turda
52 aywa, mmuy s yism n ṛbbi nnk amqqran
