tazart
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbii ad ggalɣ s **ttin** d **zzaytun** illa mad innan: tazart d uzmmur lli cttan middn? illa mad innan: idrarn g tmazirt n **lquds**? zun d **ṭṭuṛ sinin** ad t igan d udrar lli g isawl ṛbbi s **musa** d tamazirt n **makka** zɛma iggul ṛbbi s tmizar lli g d yuzn imazann nns (macc ṛbbi ka ad issnn)
2 d udrar n **sinin**
3 d tmazirt ad: lli igdl ṛbbi g kra ittukṣaḍn: **makka** lli g d yuzn ṛbbi **muḥammad** {taẓallit n ṛbbi fllas}
4 inna ṛbbi: tagallit ar nskr afgan nsastw t bahra
5 naywa nssiwrri t id s gar tigira, zɛma arwass. illa mad innan ad yussr bahra
6 bla ɣwilli flsnin sskrn mad ifulkin. ɣwi nn, llat* asn tɣrad ur tbbinin g dar ṛbbi
7 inna ṛbbi: wa afgan: ma kigan d umsskirkkis aylliɣ tɣald is ur rad yili ussḥsu g wass n tɣrɣart?
