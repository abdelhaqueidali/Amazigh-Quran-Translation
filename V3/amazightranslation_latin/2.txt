tafunast
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, **alif**-**lam**-**mim**: ṛbbi ka ad issnn mad srs iwatts?
2 ɣwad ad igan adlis, ur gis turda, iga tifawt n imkṣaḍ n ṛbbi
3 ɣwilli flsnin s untal zun d ṛbbi, d yinirn, d tɣrɣart, aywan ar ttẓallan, ar akkan ɣ ɣaylli asn nurg
4 d ɣwilli flsnin s ma nn fllak igguzn, ad t igan d **lqʷran** d mad d igguzn izwar k, d tmggarut, ssnn is d tqqan
5 ɣwinn (ɣʷin?) munn d uɣaras n ṛbbi nnsn, ɣiklli gan wid ṛwanin
6 amma ɣwilli nblnin, ha nn ingadda ukan darsn, iɣ tn tssiwdt, d iɣ tn ur tssiwdt, ha nn ur ad flsn
7 acku imsl ṛbbi wulawn nnsn, d imzgan nnsn, ig amdlu f walln nnsn, ha nn tqql srsn tmmara iggutn?
8 ha nn illa ɣ middn mad ittinin, nfls s ṛbbi, d wass amggaru, macc nttni ur gin inaflasn
9 ar ttknadn ṛbbi d willi flsnin, awa, ha nn ur ad ttknadn a bla ixfawn nnsn, sul ur as frikn?
10 acku tlla tmaḍunt ɣ wulawn nnsn, s asn issugt ṛbbi tayyaḍ, d tlla asn tmmara tamagẓaẓt, f uylli sskirkksn
11 d iɣ asn ittunna ad ur tsgufsum ɣ yiggi n wakal, nnan: ha nn ad akkʷ nga d imssawkan
12 awa, ha nn nttni s yixfawn nnsn gan igufsa, macc ur as frikn
13 d iɣ asn ittunna: aywa flsat, ɣik lli flsn middn, nnan: awa ad nfls ɣik lli flsn imṣuḍn, macc ur srs ssinn
