1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, inna ṛbbi: hann nkkni nuzn nn **nuḥ** s tamtti nns, f ad issiwd tamtti nns maḥdd ur ta tn id tucki tmmara tamagẓaẓt
2 inna **nuḥ**: wa tamtti inu, hann giɣ d srun (srwn) amssiwd amssfru
3 aywa, ssumdat i ṛbbi, tikṣuḍm t, tsgdm as
4 fad ad awn issurf ɣ ibkkaḍn nnun, issggru kʷn ar azmz lli ittussann, hann azmz n ṛbbi iɣ d yucka ur ad sul ittgru iɣ tgam wid ssnnin?
5 inna ṛbbi: wa ṛbbi inu: hann nkki ɣriɣ i tmtti inu ɣ yiḍ d uzal
6 macc tiɣri inu, ur asn tssugt bla tarwula?
7 hann nkki akud nna asn ukan ɣriɣ, f ad asn tssurft, s aywan ar tggan iḍuḍan ɣ imzgan nnsn, ḥbun ixfawn nnsn s tmlsit nnsn, aywan qqarn f ɣikann snnumɣurn bahra?
8 ɣiklli ulɣ awal inu aylliɣ asn ɣriɣ, fad ad ssfldn
9 hann nkkni ssugiɣ asn d awal inu f ad as ssfldn, ɣiklli srsn sawlɣ awal idran bahra, gr iyi disn
10 nniɣ asn: aywa, ttrat, i ṛbbi nnun, ad awn issurf, hann ntta iga amssaruf
11 ar awn d ittazan anẓaṛ iggutn ɣ ignna
12 ar awn yakka ayada, d tarwa, ar awn iskar tibḥarin*, ɣiklli awn issngi isaffn
13 maxx aylliɣ ur ad tssimɣurm ṛbbi?
14 sul iskr kʷn yad kra s kra: zɛma zɣ taḍḍit ar afgan
15 maxx is ur ad ttmnidm, manik s iskr sa ignwan gan taduliwin?
16 yaywa iskr gisn ayyur ar ismrɣa, ɣiklli iskr tafukt ar tsufu
17 hann ṛbbi ad kʷn issmɣin ɣ wakal zun d imɣi
18 ɣiklli rad kʷn srs irar, yaywa issfɣ kʷn id daɣ yan ufuɣ yaḍnin yaḍnin?
19 hann ṛbbi ifsr awn akal
20 fad ad gis ttmunm d iɣarasn lli ganin izilaln
21 inna **nuḥ**: wa ṛbbi inu: hann nttni mzarin diti, aywan ḍfuṛn walli mu ur ad issugt wayada nns d tarwa nns abla abnnan
22 hann sskundn takndawt mqqurn
23 nnan: hann ad ur tbḍum d id ṛbbi (ṛbbin) nnun, d ad ur tflm **wud** d **suwwaɛ** d **yaɣut** d **yaɛuq** d **nasr**
24 hann suḍḍṛn yad srsn bahra, ɣiklli ur ad ssigutn i inẓḍamn a ɣar tillas
25 hann ibkkaḍn nnsn a ɣ ddmn, s kcmn s lɛfit ggammin kra n turfiwin i ixfawn nnsn bla ṛbbi
26 inna **nuḥ** wa ṛbbi inu: hann riɣ gik ad ur tajjt mqqar d yan ɣ imnabaln, ɣ iggi n wakal
27 hann iɣ tn tujjit, rad uḍḍṛn ismgan nnk, ɣiklli ur rad arun bla anfruɣ amnabal
28 wa ṛbbi inu: riɣ ad iyi tssurft nkki d imarawn inu, d kra ikcmn s tgmmi inu, ig anaflas, ɣikann d inaflasn, d tnaflasin, d ad ur tssugt i inẓḍamn bla tarfafant
