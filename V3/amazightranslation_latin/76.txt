76
afgan
1 s yism n ṛbbi amallay amsmullu, hann izri yad kra n uzmz n wannaz f ufgan lli s tt inn ikkan ur ili atig
2 inna ṛbbi: hann nkkni nskr afgan ɣ ubrɣuy irɛin, fad ad t narm, naywa nskr as assfld d iẓṛi
3 hann nmla as abrid, f ad ig anaflas, nɣ amnabal?
4 hann nsmmutg i imnabaln iskraf, d tayqarin d tmẓla
5 hann ɣwilli rwanin ar ssan ɣ yan ufcku tissi immimn
6 ad t igan d yan uɣbalu ar gis ssan ismgan n ṛbbi, aywan ssngin t s aynna ran
7 ɣwinn ar jjnjumn aynna s usyin ɣiklli ttikṣuḍn, yan wass lli mu ingi uẓuḍ nns
8 d ar akkan tirmt i umẓluḍ waxxa t zuẓḍn (ẓuẓḍn), ɣikann d igigil, d wad ittyamaẓn
9 sul ar ttinin: hann ar awn nakka tirmt f udm n ṛbbi, hann ur gigun nri tiɣrad, ula tulɣitin
10 hann nkkni, nkṣuḍ a ɣar ṛbbi nnɣ ɣ yan wass ikrsn yussn
11 ɣikann a f tn yara ṛbbi f uẓuḍ n wass ann, ifk asn asmrqa n udm d tnummrt
12 ɣiklli asn ifka tiɣrad nnsn aylliɣ zziḍṛn, ad tnt igan, d ukccum s turtit, sul ig umṭṭalsu timlsit nnsn
13 aywan ẓẓaln gis f isuma, ur ad gisn qrukn i tafukt, ula asmmiḍ (aṣmmiḍ)
14 sul agʷln d fllasn iskkiḍn n turtit ann, iknu d fllasn uẓway nns, sul s ɣiklli t ran
15 ar asn ttsutuln wazzann s ifcka n ttqqurt d izɛkan smirqinin
16 ifckan ann lli smirqinin, ttuskarn ɣ nqqrt (uẓṛf) ar ttasin unck lli ssan
17 ar asn sswan inẓiṛ irɛin d tujjut
18 ad t igan d yan uɣbalu gis, ittussan s tamimt
19 ar asn tssutuln wazzann ganin imɣlal, iɣ tn tannit tɣalt tn is gan amɣwan inna zwan
20 iɣ tannit ɣ ɣinn, hann ar ttmnadt tanafit, d tkldit tabarawt
21 lsan timlsit n umṭṭalsu azigzaw (azgzaw), d usmrɣay, ɣiklli ugln tannalin n uẓṛf (nnqʷrt), sul ar asn isswa ṛbbi nnsn s anẓiṛ izddign
22 inna asn ṛbbi: hann ɣwad iga awn tiɣrad, acku askkir nnun ifulki
23 hann nkkni nzzugz nn fllak **lqʷṛan** imikk s imikk
24 awa, ẓiḍṛ i uzrf n ṛbbi nnk, hann ad ur tsgdt i ubkkaḍ gisn ula amnabal
25 aywa, bdr ism n ṛbbi nnk ɣ taylgi d tadugat
26  taywat tguntut (tgʷntut*?) as ɣ yiḍ, tmmuyt as ɣ yiḍ iɣzzifn
27 hann ɣwid ar ttirin tamzruyt, aywan ttun yan wass iẓẓayn lli tn id iqqann
28 nkkni nskr tn, nssidus askkir nnsn, iɣ nra rad tn nsnfl s mad tn irwasn
29 hann xtad tga assukti (asskʷti) i wanna iran ad imun d iɣaras n ṛbbi nns
30 hann ur ad ttirim bla iɣ ira ṛbbi, hann ṛbbi iga amssan amusnaw
31 ar isskcum wanna ira s tmllawt nns, zɛma turtit nns, ɣiklli ismmutg i inẓḍamn tammar tamagzaẓt (tamagẓaẓt)
